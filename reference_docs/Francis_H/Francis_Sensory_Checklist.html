Sensory Checklist

Daily Human-Machine Interface Audit

EXIT TO DASHBOARD

AUDIT SLUHA (SOUND)

Listen for the machine's "voice".

NORMAL
Uniform Generator 'Humm' + Water Rush
ALARM SIGNS
  • Metal Click: Loose distributor bolt/linkage.
  • Hissing: HPU valve bypass/leak.

AUDIT MIRISA (SMELL)

Detect chemical changes in the air.

OZON (THUNDERSTORM SMELL)
Indicates Corona Discharge / Insulation Failure in Stator.
BURNT OIL
Overheating Bearings or HPU pump friction.

AUDIT VIDA (SIGHT)

Verify mechanical geometry and fluid health.

  • MIV Angle: Lever arm visually at 80° (Parallel to flow).
  • Oil Color (HPU): Clear Amber. Milky = Water/Air Contamination.

AUDIT DODIRA (VIBRATION/HEAT)

Physical feedback from the machine.

HEAT THRESHOLD
Place hand on Bearing Housing coverage.
Rule: If you can't hold your hand for 5 seconds, it is > 60°C. REPORT IMMEDIATELY.
