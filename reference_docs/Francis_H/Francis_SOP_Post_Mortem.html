POST-MORTEM ANALYSIS

BACK TO DASHBOARD
W-04-WH CRITICAL

WATER HAMMER / SURGE

Rapid pressure rise (>25% baseline) indicating guide vane jam or valve malfunction. Immediate structural shock risk.

M-01-SYNC HIGH

DISTRIBUTOR SYNC ERROR

Asymmetric movement in guide vanes. Risk of shear pin breakage and hydraulic imbalance.

A-01-N2 HIGH

NITROGEN PRE-CHARGE LOSS

Pressure in HPU accumulator below operational limit. Loss of damping in emergency shutdown.

ERR_LEAK_01 MED

SHAFT SEAL LEAKAGE

Baseline leakage exceeded. Carbon ring wear or pressure imbalance in the labyrinth.

THE 3-STEP POST-MORTEM

1. SYMPTOM ISOLATION

Identify the primary code (ERR_ID) and operational context (Head, Flow, RPM) at time of failure.

2. TREND RECONSTRUCTION

Review the last 24h trend leading to the spike. Look for gradual increase vs. sudden step-change.

3. COMPONENT VALIDATION

Physically verify the component. Use DTI for mechanical vs. SCADA loop-check for instrument error.
