FIELD FLASHING (BOOTSTRAP)

Residual magnetism in large rotors is often insufficient to start the bridge. External 110V DC flashing is required at 95% rated speed.

  • 01
    Check Field Breaker (41F) is OPEN.
  • 02
    Inject 110V DC for 3-5 seconds max.
  • 03
    Confirm Voltage buildup (>80% nominal).

CRITICAL TIMER

If Field Flashing > 10s: Thermal damage to start resistors will occur. Emergency stop immediately if field does not build up after 5s injection.
