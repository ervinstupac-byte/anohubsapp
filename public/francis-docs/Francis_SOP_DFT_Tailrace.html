THE HIDDEN ENERGY THEFT

The Draft Tube recovers 30-40% of the turbine's power by creating suction. Every centimeter of "Backpressure" in the tailrace is a direct subtraction from the Net Head ($H_{net}$).

SILT IN ELBOW

In horizontal units, the 90° elbow is a sediment trap. Flow velocity drops here, allowing sand and rocks to settle.

ACTION: Enter via manhole (LOTO 01). Clear bottom sand bars. Turbulence here destroys suction efficiency.

LINER INTEGRITY

Inspect the upper 1m of the liner (closest to runner) for pitting or sponge-texture cavitation.

REPAIR: If pitting > 3mm, use Underwater Grade Concrete Repair Mortar or weld overlay.

TAILRACE CHANNEL PATROL

  • VEGETATION: Clear overgrown bushes from banks to reduce friction.
  • ROCKFALLS: Check tunnel exit for fallen boulders blocking outflow.
  • VIBRATION LIMIT: Structural vibration on DFT door must be < 2.5 mm/s RMS (ISO 10816).
