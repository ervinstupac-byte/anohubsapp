STARTUP LOGIC GATE

Sequence of Operations (SOP) Decision Tree

START
STEP 1 Verify HPU Health

Is Oil Pressure > 120 Bar?
Is Nitrogen Level OK?

YES ↓ NO → STOP
STEP 2 Distributor Status

Are Guide Vanes at ABSOLUTE 0%?
(Verify "Squeeze" Pressure)

YES ↓ NO → STOP (Leak Risk)
STEP 3 Open Bypass Valve

Command Bypass OPEN.
Wait for Spiral Case to fill.

STEP 4 Pressure Equalization

Is P_penstock - P_spiral < 1 Bar?

YES (Equalized) ↓ NO (Leak) → ABORT startup
STEP 5 Command MIV OPEN

Monitor Limit Switches.
Verify physical arm movement.

STEP 6 Initiate Speed-No-Load (SNL)

Release Brakes.
PID ramps vanes to Start position.

RUN
